2019-20 Optimized Assists (Regular Season)

Optimized Assists (Opt. AST) is a redefinition of the traditional assists stat that provides more context on the types of shots a player is assisting on. It weighs three-point assists 1.5 times more than two-point assists, and accounts for free throw assists and secondary assists. Additionally, it also adjusts for the scoring skill-level of a player’s teammates.

Passer Production Rating (P.P.R.) is a metric built off of Optimized Assists that estimates a player's production as a passer given then amount of time the spend on the ball per possession.
