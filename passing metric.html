2019-20 Optimized Assists (Regular Season)

Weighted Assists (Wght AST) is a metric that weighs a players’ assists based on the value of the shots they are creating. The weights are based on the points per shot of assist locations. Players who frequently assist on high efficiency shots (at-rim assists, corner 3PTs assists, free throw assists) benefit from Weighted Assists.
