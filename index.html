2019-20 Off-Ball Scoring Percentiles (Regular Season)

The "Cut Scoring" and "Off Screen Scoring" percentiles are estimated rankings of a player's per possession contribution in each respective area of off-ball scoring. These percentiles are calculated using publicly available tracking data and playtype data, and take into account volume, efficiency, player movement, and player role. Note: This metric only includes perimeter players (including combo forwards).
